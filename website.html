Main Dish

CHICKEN ADOBO(Grilled Chicken)

Ingredients: Chicken leg quarters, soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, black pepper, and banana ketchup for basting.

Preparation:

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, garlic, and calamansi for at least 3 hours.
  2. Prepare the grill to medium heat.
  3. Grill chicken for 10-15 mins per side, basting with ketchup mixture until charred and cooked through.

Seafood Kare-Kare

Ingredients: Shrimp, mussels, squid, peanut sauce, annatto water, bok choy, eggplant, and string beans.

Preparation:

  1. Saute garlic and onions, then add peanut sauce and annatto water.
  2. Add vegetables and simmer until tender.
  3. Add seafood last to prevent overcooking. Serve with bagoong (shrimp paste).

Dessert

Leche Flan

Ingredients: Egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, sugar (for caramel), and vanilla extract.

Preparation:

  1. Melt sugar in llaneras to create caramel.
  2. Mix yolks and milk gently (avoid bubbles). Strain into llaneras.
  3. Steam for 30-45 minutes until firm. Chill before serving.

Special Halo-Halo

Ingredients: Shaved ice, evaporated milk, ube ice cream, leche flan, sweetened beans, macapuno, and nata de coco.

Preparation:

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened fruits and beans.
  2. Fill the glass with shaved ice.
  3. Pour milk, then top with ube ice cream, leche flan, and pinipig.

Appetizer

Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients: Ground pork, minced carrots, onions, garlic, egg, and spring roll wrappers.

Preparation:

  1. Mix pork and vegetables. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Wrap small portions in wrappers and seal with water.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown and crispy.

Kwek-Kwek (Orange Quail Eggs)

Ingredients: Boiled quail eggs, flour, cornstarch, annatto powder (for orange color), and water.

Preparation:

  1. Dredge boiled eggs in flour.
  2. Dip eggs in the orange batter mixture.
  3. Deep fry until the coating is crispy. Serve with spiced vinegar.

Drinks

Pearl Milk Tea

Ingredients: Black tea, milk/creamer, sugar syrup, and tapioca pearls (boba).

Preparation:

  1. Brew tea and let it cool.
  2. Boil tapioca pearls until chewy; soak in syrup.
  3. Combine tea, milk, and ice. Shake well and add pearls to the glass.

Buko Shake (Coconut Shake)

Ingredients: Fresh coconut meat, coconut water, condensed milk, and ice.

Preparation:

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
